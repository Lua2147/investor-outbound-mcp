# Investor Targeting SOP — 5 Active Deals

## How to Use This Document
Each deal has defined search criteria across two dimensions:
1. **Title Keywords** — what the person's role should contain (searched via `contains()` on Title field)
2. **Company Targets** — specific firms whose full org charts were pulled (searched via `startswith()` on company name)

When segmenting the CSV lists for outreach, filter contacts by matching their title and/or company against the criteria below. Prioritize contacts that match BOTH a relevant title AND a relevant company.
